WHAT'S FIXED / ADDED IN THIS ZIP
────────────────────────────────
1) 📇 BARCODE — SCANNER NOW LEARNS
   • Barcodes are now stored inside customFields.barcode when you save a product
     (previously they were being dropped, so history matching never worked).
   • Whenever the same barcode is scanned again, it pulls all details from your
     history (name, unit, category, storage, location) — just enter fresh expiry.
   • Databases queried in order:
       1. Your own kitchen history (learned barcodes)
       2. Open Food Facts (food products)
       3. UPCitemDB (general retail)
       4. Open Beauty Facts (cleaning/cosmetics)
   • If none match, a friendly toast tells you it'll be remembered next time.

2) 🖨️ PRINT LOGBOOK — iOS-FRIENDLY
   • Replaced the pop-up window (which iPhones can't close) with a full-screen
     in-app modal.
   • You now have a big "← Close" button top-left AND at the bottom.
   • A green "🖨️ Print / Save PDF" button top-right prints the same beautifully
     formatted A4 sheet.
   • Uses @media print CSS to hide UI chrome — only the sheet is printed.

3) 🎤 VOICE — EMERGENCY CLOSE
   • Added a red X "Force close" button in the top-right of the Voice dialog.
   • Cancel button now does a hard reset (aborts recognition + clears all state)
     so you can never get stuck again.

4) 📧 EMAIL NOTIFICATIONS — SIGN-UP + APPROVAL
   • You now get a "🎉 Your kitchen is approved!" email when the admin approves
     you (this was missing before — the reason you didn't get a notification).
   • Better error logging on the admin sign-up email (check Vercel logs if
     something looks off).
   • NEW: Admin panel has a "📧 Test email" button (top right) — clicking it
     sends a test email to any address to verify Resend + your env vars are
     wired up correctly.

  IMPORTANT — WHY YOU DIDN'T GET APPROVAL EMAILS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
Two things to verify in Vercel → Settings → Environment Variables:

  ✔ RESEND_API_KEY          (must be set, prod env)
  ✔ SHELFWISE_ADMIN_EMAIL   (your patel.parth1966@gmail.com)

Also — Resend's default sender `onboarding@resend.dev` has a strict rule:
   In sandbox / free mode it can ONLY send to the email that owns the
   Resend account. If your owner_email OR the admin_email is different
   from that, emails silently disappear.

To confirm: log into the admin panel → click "📧 Test email" → enter
patel.parth1966@gmail.com. If it says "sent" but you don't get the mail,
your Resend account needs domain verification (or add all recipients as
verified sandbox contacts in Resend dashboard).
